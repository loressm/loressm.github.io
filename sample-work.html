Sample Work

Here are some examples of social media content I created to showcase my skills in storytelling, creativity, and strategy focused on Italian food brands targeting the US market.

Mozzareel – Caprese Reel

A dynamic 15-second Instagram Reel highlighting the authenticity and simplicity of an Italian Caprese salad, filmed in a real Italian location.
Objective: Engage food lovers by showing fresh, genuine Italian flavors.
Format: Short video with soft Italian music and English subtitles.
Call to action: “Would you like this fresh taste in your kitchen?”

Put it everywhere! Why you should include EVO Oil in your diet - Extra Virgin Olive Oil Carousel

An informative Instagram carousel designed to educate followers about the benefits of high-quality Italian extra virgin olive oil.
Structure: Hook slide, detailed info slides, and a final slide with a clear call to action.
Objective: Build brand awareness and educate consumers.

Keep Calm and Eat Clam – Instagram Post

A vibrant Instagram post featuring a delicious spaghetti alle vongole dish, with witty caption and targeted hashtags.
Objective: Increase engagement and highlight authentic Italian seafood cuisine.

Less Muscles, More Mussels – Facebook Post

A Facebook post mockup focused on fresh Italian mussels, crafted with a playful title and tailored for a slightly older demographic.
Objective: Adapt content style and tone to platform and audience.

Venice Food & Beverage – Location Shot

A photo capturing the essence of Italian lifestyle: food and drink set against the iconic backdrop of Venice.
Objective: Show potential for authentic location-based content to enhance brand storytelling.
