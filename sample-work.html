Mozzareel – Instagram Reel

A dynamic visual composition where, frame by frame, a classic Caprese salad takes shape. This 15-second reel uses a sequence of photos to highlight how simple it is to make this iconic Italian dish: mozzarella, tomato, basil, and extra virgin olive oil. The rhythm is supported by background music and the playful title “Capr-easy”, emphasizing the ease of preparation.

Objective & Strategy:
To showcase the authenticity and simplicity of Italian cuisine through fast, engaging, and repeatable content. This kind of reel can be part of a recurring content format that teaches basic recipes using the brand’s own products. The format encourages shares, boosts engagement, and emotionally connects the audience to the values of Made in Italy. Perfect for Italian food brands targeting international markets.
